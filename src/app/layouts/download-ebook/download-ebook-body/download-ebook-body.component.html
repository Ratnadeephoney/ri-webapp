Interviewer's Handbook -
an ebook from Rock Interview

Mentors at Rock Interview are essentially the guiding light for young talent in the industry as they take their initial steps to success. A big part of the mentors role is to assess candidates and help fine-tune their interviewing skills. This is done by conducting interviews that simulate an actual job interview that the candidate is preparing for.

Read through to prepare yourself so you can assess the candidate better, set the tone of an interview successfully, and ensure a mutually beneficial experience.
