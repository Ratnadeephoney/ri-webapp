An Interview resembles a real interview as closely as possible, for the purpose of providing an authentic experience for the candidate. It can help a job applicant to understand what is expected in a real job interview, and can help an applicant improve their self-presentation.

A candidate can also request for special attention to aspects of the interview process that he/she knows to be in need of improvement.

Interviews with Rock interview are recorded so that the candidate can review their performance afterward, and get specific feedback from the interviewer on aspects of the interview process and their profile that need improvement.
