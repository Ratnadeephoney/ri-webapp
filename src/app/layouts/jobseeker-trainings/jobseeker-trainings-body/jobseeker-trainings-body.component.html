Rock Interview is an emerging assessment platform for individuals to address their unique career-growth needs.

On registering with Rock Interview, candidates get assessed through a series of interviews and a unique, intelligent program. The program uses a combination of data science and AI on the data sets to pair the candidate with real-life professionals based on their profile and competence.

The Rock Interview platform gives candidates an opportunity for some peer-to-peer training that offers guidance and insightful feedback without the fear of being ridiculed.
